Our Story

ABC High School was founded in 1985 with the vision of providing quality education to students from all walks of life. Over the years, we have grown into a hub of academic excellence and holistic development.

Our Mission

To nurture creativity, critical thinking, and leadership among students while fostering a love for lifelong learning.

Our Vision

Empowering students to become global citizens and responsible leaders in a rapidly changing world.
